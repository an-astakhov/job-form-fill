- quantitative finance developer

- people-management-heavy roles where direct formal line-management evidence is essential

## Domain And Tailoring Notes

### Credit Risk

- Emphasize simulation-based credit-risk solutions, valuation-oriented modeling, impairment and expected-loss adjacent work, correlation models, quantitative tooling, and decision-support delivery.

### Climate Risk

- Emphasize climate-adjusted PD and financials, NGFS-style scenario work, physical climate-risk portfolio analytics, geospatial exposure data, and links between environmental and financial risk.

### Applied AI

- Emphasize orchestrated LLM-agent workflows, evaluation harnesses, reproducibility checks, practical automation, and the ability to turn multiple technical outputs into usable business insight.

### Senior Data Science / ML

- Emphasize predictive ML frameworks, production-quality code, experimentation-minded delivery, large structured datasets, and collaboration with stakeholders beyond the data-science function.
